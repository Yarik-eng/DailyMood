About DailyMood

DailyMood is a small project aimed at helping people log and reflect on their daily emotional states. We focus on a simple interface and gentle insights to encourage reflection.

The Team

A tiny, passionate group: designers, developers, and mental health advocates. We're dedicated to building tools that support everyday wellbeing.
